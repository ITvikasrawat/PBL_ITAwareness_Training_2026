🏢 Pillar 1: Office Discipline & Security

Aapka physical behavior aur workspace company ki image define karta hai.

Professionalism & Workspace ✨

1
Arrive on time: Office time par aana discipline dikhata hai.
2
Keep desk clean: Apni desk organized rakhein; unnecessary papers hata dein.
3
Daily Habit: Clean desk & clear screen policy
4
Wear ID Card: Apna ID card visibly pehne; anjaan logon se puchen.
5
No Escort, No Entry: Visitors ko akele allow na karein.

Handling Info & Access 🤫

6
Be Professional: Auditors ke sath professional communication rakhein.
7
No Public Talk: Company matters ko lift ya cafeteria mein discuss na karein.
8
Secure Disposal: Confidential printouts ko securely shred karein.
9
NO TAILGATING: Agar aap card swipe karke ghus rahe hain, toh piche aane wale kisi shaks ke liye darwaza hold na karein.

🔐 Pillar 2: System Access & Passwords

Apne digital workspace aur identity ko secure rakhna.

Workstation Security 💻

10
Lock System: Seat chhodte hi workstation turant lock karein (Win + L).
11
Hide Files: Screen par confidential files open chhodkar apni seat se na uthein.
12
No Personal Devices: Company portals me login ke liye personal devices use na karein.
13
Least Privilege: Apne role ke hisaab se sirf required data hi access karein.

Password Hygiene (Audit Focus) 🔑

14
Never Share: Passwords kabhi kisi se share na karein.
15
Strong & Unique: Har account ke liye unique passwords banayein.
16
NO STICKY NOTES: Passwords ko sticky notes ya diaries par bilkul na likhein!
17
Leak Suspected? Immediately change karein.
18
Use MFA: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) zaroor use karein.

📁 Pillar 3: Data Management & Storage

ISO 27001 Guidelines for handling company and client data.

Where to Store Data? ☁️

19
Approved Devices: Official work ke liye sirf company-approved devices use karein.
20
Approved Servers: Company files ko cloud ya approved servers par save karein.
21
Strictly Prohibited: Sensitive data ko personal mobiles ya pen drives mein store na karein.
22
Print/Copy: Confidential data ko download ya print sirf approval ke sath karein.
23
Clear Desk: Sensitive documents ko openly visible na chhodein.

Data Housekeeping & BCP 🧹

24
Shredding: Obsolete printouts ko securely shred karein.
25
Clean Folders: Shared folders se duplicate data regularly remove karein.
26
Save Work: Important work ko recovery ke liye approved locations par save karein.
27
Official Mail: Communication sirf professional accounts se karein.
28
BCP Basics: Outages ke time Business Continuity Plan (BCP) ko samjhein.

🌐 Pillar 4: Network & Device Policy

Cyber threats se apne network aur system ko bachana.

Networks & USB Policy 🔌

29
Public Wi-Fi: Public Wi-Fi par official work sirf approved VPN ke sath karein.
30
Remote Work: Secure connections use karein.
31
No Unknown USBs: Unknown USB drives ko office system se bilkul connect na karein.
32
Approved Tools: Sirf IT-approved collaboration tools use karein.
33
Phishing Alert: Unexpected email attachments ko caution ke sath verify karein.

Vishing & Prevention 🛡️

34
VISHING (Voice Phishing): Agar koi call par khud ko "German Head Office IT" bata kar aapse login ya AnyDesk maange, toh bina IT se verify kiye details na dein!
35
Never disable Firewalls manually.
36
Follow-up plan ready rakhein.
37
Emergency contact accessible rakhein.

🚨 Pillar 5: Incident Response & Audits

When things go wrong, or when Auditors are here!

System Health & GDPR Reporting 📢

38
Updates: Antivirus aur OS ko updated rakhein.
39
Restarts: System restarts sirf tabhi postpone karein jab bahut zaroori ho.
40
Incident? Apna password turant change karein.
41
Report Loss: Lost laptops/phones immediately report karein.
42
THE 72-HOUR RULE: Data breach hone par Client ko 72 hours mein notify karna hota hai. Galti hone par IT ko 5 mins mein batayein!

Facing the Auditor (Crucial) 🎯

43
No Assumptions: Audits mein galat info na dein. Answer nahi pata toh bole: "I will check the policy and get back."
44
Role Clear: Disruption ke doran aapka role kya hai, clear rahein.
45
Live the Policy: Company policy daily follow karein.
46
Shared Responsibility: ISO 27001 Security sabki responsibility hai.

Pillar 6: Project GDP-207 & GDPR

German Client Visit ke liye sabse Critical Guidelines.

GDP-207 Access Protocol 🌐

47
Strict URL & eToken: Hum German client system ko ek specific secure URL aur eTokens (OTP based login) ke through access karte hain.
48
Zero Sharing Policy: Apna eToken device ya OTP kisi bhi colleague ke sath share karna sakht mana hai. Yeh ek major security breach hai.

European Data Privacy (GDPR)

49
Data Minimization: Sirf utna hi personal data extract/process karein jitna task ke liye zaroori ho. Faltu data na nikalein.
50
Privacy Breach: Kisi bhi European citizen ki information ko unapproved local PC par save karna ek criminal offense maana jata hai.
