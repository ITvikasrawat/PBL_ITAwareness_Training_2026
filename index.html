Step 2: Review Guidelines

Please read these points carefully. The quiz is based on this.

1. IT Security Basics
  • Passwords: Use strong passwords (8+ chars). Never share them.
  • Lock Screen: Press Windows + L every time you leave your seat.
  • Phishing: Don't click suspicious links or download attachments.
2. Data Privacy
  • Storage: Save files only on the Server/OneDrive.
  • Confidentiality: Never share client data on WhatsApp/personal email.
  • Clean Desk: Do not leave sensitive papers open on your desk.
3. Workplace Conduct
  • POSH: Zero tolerance for harassment. Report incidents to HR/IC immediately.
  • Respect: Maintain professionalism in all communications.
  • Behavior: Avoid inappropriate jokes or comments.
